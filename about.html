Jericho B Yantz

The Role that I decided to take on for this project was to make and design the website. For this, I learned the basics of HTML, made some designs of what I wanted to website to generally look like, then got to work. I made sure to have spaces for links to each game as well as descriptions of the games themselves. I also made sure that there was a page dedicated to the members of this group (this page) and all the hard work we have put in. As I got thinking more about the website, I wanted to keep our project organized. To do this, I decided we should have a “Project Documents” and a “Projects Schedule” page so all the information would be together. The “Project Documents” page is complete with links to download the necessary PowerPoints or Word Docs and is being updated as we complete more documents. I hope you are enjoying the website as well as the games with it!
